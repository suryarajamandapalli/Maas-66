OUR STORY

Building Tomorrow's Infrastructure Today

MAAS 66 is a leading infrastructure company specializing in underground fiber solutions and comprehensive utility services across North America.

Founded with a vision to revolutionize infrastructure development, we have grown into a trusted partner for telecommunications, energy, and utility companies nationwide. Our commitment to excellence, safety, and innovation has made us a leader in the industry.

With decades of combined experience, our team delivers projects that connect communities, power economies, and build the foundation for future generations.

Our Mission

To deliver world-class infrastructure solutions that connect communities, power progress, and build a sustainable future for generations to come.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted infrastructure partner, recognized for our innovation, safety excellence, and positive impact on the communities we serve.

Our Values

Safety first. Integrity always. Excellence in everything. These core values guide every decision we make and every project we undertake.
