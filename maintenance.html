Maintenance | Maas 66

Maintaining the Strength and Reliability of Critical Infrastructure

Maintenance is a vital part of keeping communication networks strong, stable, and ready for everyday demands. Our team ensures that all structures, equipment, and pathways remain safe, compliant, and fully operational through proactive care and rapid response services

We handle everything from on-call construction support and pole replacement to emergency restoration and storm remediation. Whether addressing structural issues, resolving outages, or restoring damaged infrastructure, our maintenance operations are engineered for speed, accuracy, and long-term reliability

On-Call Construction Services

On-call construction services provide fast, reliable support for urgent field needs. We respond quickly to address site issues, perform repairs, and complete essential construction tasks, ensuring projects stay on schedule and networks operate without disruption

Pole Replacement & Removal

We replace and remove poles safely and efficiently, addressing structural issues before they impact service. Our team manages everything from excavation to installation, ensuring strong, stable, and compliant support for your network

Emergency Restoration

We provide emergency restoration services to repair network damage caused by accidents, weather, or unexpected failures. Our team works around the clock to restore connectivity and stabilize operations as quickly as possible
