Construction | Maas 66

Building Tomorrow's Infrastructure Today

Founded with a vision to revolutionize infrastructure development, we have grown into a trusted partner for telecommunications, energy, and utility companies nationwide. Our commitment to excellence, safety, and innovation has made us a leader in the industry.

With decades of combined experience, our team delivers projects that connect communities, power economies, and build the foundation for future generations.

OSP Aerial & Underground Cable Installation

We provide reliable OSP aerial and underground cable installation services designed for long-term performance. Our team ensures precise routing, safe installation, and strong connectivity across all terrains, supporting both new builds and network upgrades

Underground New Construction Micro-Trenching / Conduit & Manhole Installation

Our underground construction services include micro-trenching, conduit systems, and manhole builds designed for modern fiber networks. We focus on accuracy, structural strength, and safe installation to support high-capacity connectivity

Cable Removal / Wreck-out

We provide safe and efficient cable removal services, clearing outdated or damaged lines from aerial and underground routes. Our wreck-out process ensures clean pathways for new installations while maintaining environmental and safety standards

Equipment Installation & Wiring

We handle the installation and wiring of network equipment with precision and care. Our team ensures proper mounting, clean cable management, and reliable connections so your systems operate smoothly and efficiently from day one

Power & Communication Space

Our services include setting up dedicated power and communication areas with correct grounding, structured cabling, and organized equipment layouts. Each space is built for stability, safety, and optimal network performance

Splicing & Activation

We provide precise fiber splicing and activation services to ensure strong, low-loss connections. Our team handles testing, verification, and network turn-up, delivering reliable performance from the moment your system goes live

Fiber Optic Splicing

Our fiber optic splicing services include fusion splicing, buffer preparation, loss testing, and final validation. We ensure every joint meets strict performance standards for seamless data transmission across the network

Troubleshooting

We provide fast and accurate troubleshooting in fiber construction, identifying faults, signal loss, and structural issues. Our team uses advanced testing tools to locate problems quickly and restore network performance with minimal downtime

Network Support

We take care of your network, fix problems fast, and make sure everything stays connected and working smoothly every day
